History of the Blues

Keep The Blue Flag Fly High


Chelsea Football Club, commonly referred to as Chelsea, is a professional football club based in Fulham, London. Founded in 1905, the club has a rich history and is one of the most successful football clubs in England. Chelsea has won numerous domestic and international titles, including multiple English Premier League titles, FA Cups, and UEFA Champions League trophies. The club's home ground is Stamford Bridge, which has a seating capacity of over 40,000 spectators. Chelsea is known for its passionate fan base and has a strong rivalry with other London clubs such as Arsenal and Tottenham Hotspur. The club's colors are blue and white, and they are nicknamed "The Blues" or "The Pensioners." Over the years, Chelsea has been home to many legendary players and managers, contributing to its status as one of the top football clubs in the world.
